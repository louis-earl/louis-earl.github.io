About the Project

Valocity Onsite is a tool designed for Property Valuers to efficiently record data during their on-site inspection.

My Involvement

I crafted the UI/UX designs - An overhaul of an existing app, redesigned from the ground up. - The company’s first in-house design project. - Not to mention my first major design project. - Learnings and takeaways.

How it started

Turning problems into opportunity

The old Valocity Connect app had low usage. Valuers told us it was faster to complete their site inspections using pencil and paper, and then manually re-enter that data on Valocity’s desktop platform back at the office.

Investigating user pain points

While we had the opportunity to start from the ground up, it was important to learn from past mistakes to provide a more useful and useable experience. Job discovery: Navigation of large forms Excessive form fields Difficult field validations

Design Iterations

Customer Feedback

Rearranging the flow based on physical investigations.

Introduction of the design system

Some small updates to the UI made a major improvement to the look and feel of Onsite. Better UI guidelines provided stronger standards for our use of fonts and colours - with accessibility in mind.
