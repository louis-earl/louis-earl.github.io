About Me

Hello! I’m Louis (pronounced Louie, but I’ll answer either way!). I’m currently living in Wellington and have called this place home for the last three years.
To be honest, I rarely dive into the black hole that is my own values and identity - so I struggle sometimes to put this into tangible writing. It’s been so fluid for me over the past few years (and I hope it continues to be!). I’ve pinpointed a few values I’m currently being guided by: self-care, friendship/family, respect, enjoyment and achievement.
I wanted to talk about self-care as one of my most inner core values - and I hope to explain this is separated from selfishness as much as possible! By practising self-care and replenishing myself internally, especially on a mental health level, I’m able to have so much more to share with others externally. I picture this is a bit like filling up a jug of water - the fuller the jug, the more cups I can fill up, too. I believe the best of my core values all stem from self-care - so by nurturing self-care, my other values can all flourish.
I’m attending Dev Academy because I have a love for programming! I started out trying to study software engineering in Christchurch, but very quickly decided this wasn’t for me (I found the maths horrific). I then moved to Welly in 2017 and started my (now completed) Bachelor of Creative Media Production at Massey. At EDA, I’m hoping to widen my career options with some more employable skillsets - a step in the right direction for me!
